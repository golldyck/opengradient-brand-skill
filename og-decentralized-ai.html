How It Works

From call to verified result in milliseconds

01

Your App Calls the Contract

Send an inference request via smart contract. Specify model ID, input, max fee, and SLA. Funds are escrowed atomically.

og.infer(modelId, input, { maxFee: "0.001 ETH" })
02

Validators Race to Respond

GPU validators pick up the job. They execute the model, generate a cryptographic attestation, and submit the signed result on-chain.

result + zkAttestation → L2
03

Result Settled On-Chain

Consensus is reached. Escrow releases to the winning validator. Your contract receives the verified output. Dispute period: 15 minutes.
