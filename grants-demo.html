Funding Tracks

Three tracks.
One mission.

Whether you're deploying a proof of concept or scaling a production protocol, there's a track for you.

$50K
Explorer Grant
For individual developers and early-stage teams exploring decentralized AI applications.
  • Open source deliverable
  • Individual or team ≤ 3
  • 3-month milestone timeline
  • On-chain inference required

Builder Grant
For teams building production infrastructure, tooling, or applications on OpenGradient.
  • Working prototype required
  • Defined go-to-market plan
  • 6-month delivery scope
  • Technical advisor pairing
Rolling Review
$500K
Research Grant
For academic groups and research labs advancing verifiable inference and decentralized ML theory.
  • Published research background
  • 12-month research scope
  • Publication deliverable
  • Invite or referral only

Process

From idea to funded
in four steps.

01
Apply Online
Submit your project concept, team background, and proposed use of OpenGradient infrastructure.
02
Technical Review
Our engineering team evaluates feasibility, originality, and network fit within 7 business days.
03
Committee Vote
Approved applications go to our grants committee — a live call with founders and domain experts.
04
Funds Deployed
Grant disbursed on-chain against milestone completions. Ongoing support from our ecosystem team.
